STEPHANIE HART

I am an educator who is constantly seeking opportunities to improve communities and processes through training and design. From idea development to assessment, from marketing to training, I love being involved in all aspects of a project and seeing a creative vision come to life. For me there's nothing more motivating that a brand new planner and nothing more satisfying than a finished to-do list. I am inspired to focus on the details of built experiences, knowing that small actions accumulate into entire movements. I thrive when I can channel my creative energy into human centered projects that have a positive impact on society. When I'm not developing engaging student programs or facilitating professional development workshops I'm teaching myself digital design and illustration or working on my '72 Yamaha XS-2.

Life Extremes Game

Interactive Game for College Student Decision Making



Card game designed to present life choices on each extreme of a spectrum. College students are asked to draw one of each card type (green: work, purple: lifestyle, red: relationships) and build their preferred life from their options. The game is designed to help students think through how they want to live after college and consider the sacrifices they may need to make in order to be who they want to be.

Career Options Cards

Interactive Cards for College Student Career Exploration


Career Cards with descriptions and industries from ONet Online. Cards are used in an interactive activity where students draw three cards at random and are asked to choose which career they would be most interested in. Additional questions are asked to deepen understandings of career options including, “How does this position contribute positively to society?”; “What negative contributions might be possible in this position?”
